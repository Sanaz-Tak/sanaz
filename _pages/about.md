I am an Assistant Teaching Professor of [Engineering Design](https://www.sedi.psu.edu/) at Penn State University and hold a Ph.D. in [Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.eecs.psu.edu/) from [Penn State University](https://www.psu.edu/). My research focuses on brain-inspired and neuromorphic computing systems, with my doctoral work centered on developing and optimizing spatio-temporal neural networks for computer vision and robotics—particularly for efficient deployment on low-power edge computing platforms. I also hold an M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering with a concentration in Control Systems Theory from the [University of Calgary](https://schulich.ucalgary.ca/electrical-software), and a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering with a concentration in Electronics from [Tabriz University](https://ece.tabrizu.ac.ir/en). My interdisciplinary expertise spans neuromorphic computing, computer vision, robotics, and control systems, and I bring experience from both academia and industry, especially in AI/ML training frameworks and advanced control algorithms.

![](../images/PointLCA.png)
Treats Dynamic Vision Sensor event streams as 3D point clouds, pairing PointNet feature extraction with an Exemplar LCA encoder-decoder. Achieves **94.41% on DVS128, 99.01% on NMNIST, and 78.46% on SHD**, with up to **99.54% less inference computation** and **0.065 mJ** per inference on DVS128 — well suited to energy-constrained robotics and UAVs.

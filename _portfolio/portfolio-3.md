A scalable Exemplar LCA encoder-decoder that builds its dictionary directly from training features, eliminating costly dictionary training and error backpropagation. Reaches top-1 accuracies of **99.99% (MNIST), 94.98% (CIFAR-10), 79.32% (CIFAR-100), and 80.79% (ImageNet)**, with inference as low as 0.25 GFLOPs and support for incremental new examples — ready for memristor-based crossbar arrays. [📄 Read the published article](https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/2634-4386/ad9e2c)
